What we do

Full-service wrap,
from strike to re-rent

Every service your production needs once the shoot ends — handled by one specialized team.

Set Strike & Removal

Skilled crews dismantle flats, walls, platforms, scenic builds, and set dressing — safely, quickly, on your schedule.

Inventory & Cataloging

Every item tagged, photographed, and entered into a searchable digital catalog — so you (and future productions) can find it instantly.

Warehouse Storage

Organized by category — walls, doors, props, furniture, specialty builds. Everything stored clean and accessible in our LA facility.

Rental Program

Rent reclaimed set materials back to film, TV, commercial, and event productions. High-quality assets at a fraction of build cost.

Cartage & Logistics

On-demand trucking from production sites to our warehouse, and delivery to new productions anywhere in our service area.

Waste & Recycling

We sort what's reusable from what's scrap, responsibly disposing of and recycling materials that can't be given a second life.

The process

Four steps, zero headaches

From the moment a shoot wraps to the moment your next production calls — we handle every step.

1
You call wrap

Contact us before or during production. We'll schedule your strike crew around your timeline.

2
We strike the set

Our crew dismantles, loads, and transports everything — walls, props, furniture — to our warehouse.

3
Assets get cataloged

Every piece is photographed, tagged, and added to our searchable inventory database.

4
Assets earn revenue

Items rent out to future productions — generating ongoing income on materials you'd otherwise discard.
